Investment Thesis

We are a sector agnostic fund, backing entrepreneurs at early stages of their venture
with cheque sizes to the tune of $100k - $150k.


The Gap:

Startups generally tend to fail due to three major reasons -

Lack of capital
Weak tech/product
Inability to scale efficiently
The Solution:

With tech being a prerequisite, we believe if we are able to plug the gap of capital and scale, we could drastically improve the startup’s chances of success. We work actively with our portfolio companies to help them leverage our network in the ecosystem and achieve growth metrics effectively


Our Approach

The breadth of support we provide to our startups is unique for an early stage investor. It spans from Hiring, Marketing, Operations to Product & Growth
